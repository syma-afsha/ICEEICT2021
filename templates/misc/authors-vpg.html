Video Presentation Guidelines

Guidelines For Video Presentation

Welcome to ICEEICT 2021 Video Presentation Guidelines.
Before you proceed for submission, you are requested to go through the guidelines for presenting the video in the following sections.

  • The duration of a presentation slot is 15 minutes. Author will have 10 minutes for the presentation and 5 minutes for questions and answers.

  • Prepare a presentation on PowerPoint for 10 minutes based on the template.

  • Prepare a 10 minute video in MP4 format (maximum 100 MB). Make the video according to the following instructions:

  • Go to Zoom.us from your computer

  • Sign in to the application with your credentials.

  • Host a new meeting with video.

  • Start a new meeting and then join with computer audio.

  • Activate the camera.

  • Make your zoom window fullscreen.

  • Click the " Record " in ZOOM to start the video presentation.

  • Click the " Share Screen " in Zoom.

  • Click your already opened PowerPoint presentation file.

  • Go to the slide presentation mode and then start your presentation.

  • Give your concluding remarks at the end of the presentation and click on " Stop Share" on zoom.

  • Click the " Stop Recording " in Zoom.

  • Click " End Meeting " -> " End Meeting for all " in Zoom.

  • Please wait some time for converting your video presentation into a .mp4 file. After completing the conversion process, the file is saved automatically to your computer.

  • Rename the file according to the following file naming conversion PaperID.mp4 (where PaperID is the Paper ID of your paper ).

  • Upload the video presentation on CMT.
