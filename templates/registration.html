Registration Form Submission


For Participants/ Non-presenting Authors:

To register as a non-presenting author/participant, see the registration and payment fees given in the section below and complete the payment.

ICEEICT Organizing Team is pleased to deliver the conference kit/souvenir to a registered author's/participant's address upon the payment of additional BDT 200.00 for a local address and USD 85.00 (considering a fixed exchange rate) for an international address.

After the payment, you should have the following documents ready to complete the registration form in the given link below:

  • Proof of nationality, studentship, IEEE membership (if applicable)
  • Payment slip


For Conference Delegates (Session Chairs / Track Chairs / Reviewers):

This category registration requires a prior invitation sent from the ICEEICT TPC.

To register as a conference delegate (Session Chair / Track Chair / Reviewer ), please fill up the necessary registration information in the form below. Please select your role as "Session Chair " , "Track Chair " or "Reviewer", as appropriate.

        Deadline: 30 OCT


For Presenting Authors:

The registered authors now need to complete the remaining steps for the Camera-ready documents submission from their following CMT authors' portal.
