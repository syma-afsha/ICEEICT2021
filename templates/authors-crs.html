Camera Ready Submission Guideline For ICEEICT 2021

Welcome to ICEEICT 2021 Camera Ready Submission Guideline.


Camera-ready Documents Submission (Deadline: 30 Nov 2021 )

  • Step 1 : Camera Ready Preparation
  • Step 2 : IEEE PDF eXpress
  • Step 3 : IEEE eCF (Electronic Copyright) Form
  • Step 4 : Required Document Submission

Step 1: Camera Ready Preparation

You are required to revise your manuscript carefully addressing the comments of the reviewers and do thorough proofreading of the manuscript.

Please strictly follow the IEEE type-setting (i.e., title, section headings, body-texts, and figures and tables with their captions) and bibliographic requirements to avoid any ground for delaying the submission of ICEEICT 2021 papers to IEEE Xplore.

Ensure that your revised manuscript still retains the criteria of the initial paper submission below.
  1. 1. File size: 3 MB

  2. 2. Paper Size: A4

  3. 3. Paper length: 4 to 6 pages including all figures, tables, and references

  4. 4. Fonts: Type 3 fonts are not allowed. All fonts are to be embedded in the PDF file.
For the paper with similarity score higher than 30% (report sent by email), the authors should revise their paper to reduce the score below 30% and the similarity from a single source below 3%. Please note that it remains solely the authors responsibility if IEEE does not approve the paper on this similarity ground.

Conference Name

Add the conference name on the top left corner of the first page of your manuscript as shown in the sample page given below.

2021 5th International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Information & Communication Technology (ICEEICT)
Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Dhaka-1216, Bangladesh


Copyright Code

The appropriate copyright clearance code notice is to appear on the bottom of the first page of each paper according to the guidelines set forth in the Cataloging/Copyright Instructions for an IEEE Conference Proceeding. Detailed instructions can be found at:
https://www.ieee.org/publications/rights/index.html

For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government, the copyright notice is: U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright

For papers in which all authors are employed by a Crown government (UK, Canada, and Australia), the copyright notice is: 978-1-6654-9522-6/21/$31.00 ©2021 Crown

For papers in which all authors are employed by the European Union, the copyright notice is:
978-1-6654-9522-6/21/$31.00 ©2021 European Union

For all other papers, whose authors are employed in Bangladesh the copyright notice is:
978-1-6654-9522-6/21/$31.00 ©2021 IEEE

Step 1 : Presentation

Slide presentation guideline

You will have 10 minutes for the presentation and 5 minutes for questions and answers. Thus, the total duration of a presentation slot is 15 minutes. Download the presentation slide template given below and start preparing your presentation based on the template. Rename the *.pptx filename with your paper ID (i.e., paper_id.pptx).


The presentation slide/file is to be uploaded in CMT (Author portal) during camera-ready document submission.


Video presentation guideline

  • Prepare a 10 minute video in MP4 format (maximum 100 MB) with a voice narrated presentation and inset video of the presenter via zoom or similar platforms.
    The presenter's video is to be kept at the bottom right corner of the screen (inset) during recording and must ensure that it does not obstruct the contents of the slides.
    Name the video file as “Paper_id_video.mp4”.
    The video file is to be uploaded in CMT (Author portal) during camera-ready document submission.
