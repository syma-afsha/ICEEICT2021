Special Technical Sessions

Session Title

Imaging and next generation networks for smart applications (iNGN4SmartApp)


Session Chairs

  • Brigadier General A K M Nazrul Islam
  • Md Golam Mostafa
  • Lieutenant Colonel Hussain Md Abu Nyeem
  • M. Shamim Kaiser

About the Special Session

Smart technologies have an increasing and remarkable impact on today’s life-hood, society, research, and industry with its enormous promises for a spectrum of emerging applications. Particularly, it widens the scope of research and development with the acquisition, processing, analysis and prediction, and presentation of the information to make this information actionable for users and to improve experiences. A high-quality user experience with real- time big data for such smart apps further require research and development on higher capacity and greatly lower latency transmission networks. The rapid growth of visual information also demands innovative systems that gather huge amounts of data from sensors and other sources, using machine learning algorithms and predictive analytics for the real-world applications with many dynamic solutions and new opportunities to reshape the entire industries.

In support of engaging a timely discussion on the advances of smart applications, a special session is included in the ICEEICT technical program. This session primarily focuses on new smart applications of imaging and next generation of network capturing intelligent, contextual, proactive, cooperative, and distant systems and services in different areas including medical, space, military, security, surveillance, communication, control etc.

Topics of Interest: specially focused but not limited to:

  • Quantum Cognitive Imaging and Neural Networks
  • Computer Vision and High-speed Network for Healthcare Application
  • Robotic vision, and Low latency network for Remote patient monitoring and Surgery
  • Multi- und Hyperspectral Image Communication
  • Multimedia Networking and Traffic Management
  • Smart Motion Estimation, Registration, and Fusion
  • Deep Learning for Images and Videos
  • Multi-view, 3D, and Computational Imaging
  • Integration of Imaging Informatics & Bioinformatics
  • Low Delay and Multipath Video Communication
  • Green Communication System and Networks
  • Fog/Edge Computing and Big data System with 5G and beyond
  • Resource Allocation for Next generation Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence and 6G Networks for Smart Applications
  • Visible Light Communication for 6G
  • Smart Control, Sensor and Actuator Networks
  • Medical Imaging and High Speed Network
  • Applications and Implementations of 6G Internet of Things
  • Biometrics, Forensics, and Security
  • Wearables, Body Sensor Networks, and Smart Portable Devices
  • Emerging Smart Applications and Systems
