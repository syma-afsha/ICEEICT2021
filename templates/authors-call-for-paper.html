Call for papers

Department of Electrical, Electronic and Communication Engineering (EECE) , Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) invites you to participate in the International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Information Communication Technology (ICEEICT) which is going to be held on 18-20th Nov 2021. ICEEICT aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers, scholars to exchange and share their experiences, research results about all aspects of Electrical Engineering, ICT and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted.




Scopes

Track 1 : Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics
  1. Big Data Analytics
  2. Distributed and Cloud Computing
  3. Reconfigurable Computing
  4. Human-Computer Interaction
  5. Machine Learning and Expert Systems
  6. Mobile Computing
  7. Neural Network and Fuzzy Logic
  8. Pattern Recognition
  9. Robotics and Automation


Track 2 : Biomedical Engineering
  1. Biomedical Devices and Instrumentations
  2. Biosensors, Bioelectronics and Bioinformatics
  3. Biomechanics and Rehabilitation
  4. Biomedical Imaging and Signal Processing
  5. Cognitive Engineering
  6. Healthcare Engineering


Track 3 : Computer Network and Security
  1. Computer Architecture
  2. Cyber-Physical System
  3. Cryptography
  4. Data Communication and Networking
  5. Distributed and Network Security
  6. Internet of Things (IoT)


Track 4 : Communication Technology

  3. Multimedia Communication

Track 5 : Power Electronics and Drives

Track 6 : Power System and Renewable Energy
  1. Distributed Generation
